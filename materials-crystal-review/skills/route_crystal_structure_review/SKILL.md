title: Route Crystal Structure Review
description: Route materials questions through structure, symmetry, and readiness experts.
---

Use this skill at the root of a materials crystal review. Delegate first to
structure inspection, then request symmetry or simulation-readiness judgment
when the user asks whether the structure can be trusted or reused.

The final answer should identify the structure, evidence, caveats, and next
actions.
