title: Require Delivery Form Before Derivation
description: Require known terrain delivery form before choosing tools.
---

Use this skill before terrain derivation. Confirm whether the input is a raw
point cloud, gridded point CSV, raster DEM, or catalog-only result.

If delivery form is unknown, ask the data/collector path to resolve it before
running terrain tools.
