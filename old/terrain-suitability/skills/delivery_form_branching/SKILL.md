---
name: delivery_form_branching
title: Delivery Form Branching
description: Choose terrain processing path based on delivery form.
---

Use this skill when terrain input may be a catalog record, LAZ/LAS point cloud,
CSV point cloud, raster DEM, or precomputed grid. Route raw point clouds through
gridding and DEMs through terrain derivation.

Do not call DEM tools directly on raw point-cloud files.
