name: recover_from_catalog_failures
title: Recover From Catalog Failures
description: Recover agentically from NDP catalog or staging failures.
---

Use this skill when catalog search, resource resolution, or staging fails.
Return structured context to the parent and consider alternate candidates,
alternate organizations, direct public URLs, or a bounded fallback source.

Do not stop at a bare "download failed" message when recovery paths exist.
