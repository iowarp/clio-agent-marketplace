---
name: check_coordinate_bounds
title: Check Coordinate Bounds
description: Check coordinate bounds and spatial plausibility.
---

Use this skill when reviewing vector or tabular geospatial data. Inspect
coordinate ranges, CRS hints, longitude/latitude ordering, impossible values,
and bounding boxes.

Report whether the coordinates are plausible for the stated region. If CRS is
missing, keep conclusions conditional and recommend a CRS confirmation.
