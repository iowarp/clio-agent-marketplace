---
name: flag_heterozygosity_outliers
title: Flag Heterozygosity Outliers
description: Flag samples with suspicious heterozygosity metrics.
---

Use this skill after per-sample heterozygosity or het/hom evidence is available.
Compare samples to cohort context and flag excess or deficient heterozygosity
as possible contamination, inbreeding, sample swap, or low-quality data.

Do not flag from one metric alone when cohort context is missing.
