title: Identify Contig And GC Evidence
description: Identify contig, length, and GC-content evidence in references.
---

Use this skill when summarizing a reference FASTA or assembly. Record contig
names, lengths, total bases, GC content, ambiguous bases, and unusual contig
patterns.

Use these facts to support reference-readiness claims.
