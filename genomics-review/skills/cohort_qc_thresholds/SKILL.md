title: Cohort QC Thresholds
description: Apply cohort QC threshold reasoning to per-sample variant metrics.
---

Use this skill when interpreting per-sample cohort QC metrics. Consider call
rate, missingness, heterozygosity, sex-chromosome evidence, and outlier status.

Treat thresholds as decision support, not absolute truth. Explain which metrics
triggered a flag and what follow-up would confirm it.
