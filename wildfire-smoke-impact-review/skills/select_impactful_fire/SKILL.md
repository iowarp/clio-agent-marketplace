---
name: select_impactful_fire
title: Select Impactful Fire
description: Choose the active fire that is actually affecting people downwind.
---

Use this skill when several active fires are in play and one must be chosen.
Impact is the intersection of three live sources: a fire, smoke forecast over
its region, and air-quality monitors showing degraded readings under that smoke.

Rank candidates by how much populated, monitored area sits under their smoke
footprint with elevated AQI. A large but contained or smoke-free fire is not
impactful. If no candidate overlaps monitored population, conclude that there is
no significant downwind impact right now.
