title: Coordinate Wildfire Impact Review
description: Route a wildfire downwind-impact question through acquisition, region, impact, map, and brief.
---

Use this skill at the root of a wildfire smoke-impact review. Drive the domain
experts on the typed evidence they return, not on the wording of their prose.

Acquire active fires first (they define the region), derive the region, then
acquire smoke and air quality over it. Select the fire by downwind impact — a
fire whose smoke overlaps populated air-quality monitors — never by acreage.
When impact is present, ensure a map is rendered before the brief. When no fire
is putting smoke over monitored population, report that null result honestly
instead of forcing a map.
