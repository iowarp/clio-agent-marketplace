name: coordinate_terrain_site_suitability
title: Coordinate Terrain Site Suitability
description: Coordinate terrain discovery, derivation, suitability, and visualization.
---

Use this skill at the root of a terrain-suitability workflow. Determine whether
the input is catalog metadata, raw point cloud, gridded DEM, or ready terrain
derivative, then delegate to the correct child.

Final synthesis should include provenance, derivative method, constraints, and
candidate site evidence.
